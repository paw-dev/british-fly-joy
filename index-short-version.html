Frequently Asked Questions About Fly&Joy

Is Fly&Joy ATOL protected?

Yes. Fly&Joy UK owns an ATOL certification issued by the Civil Aviation Authority, which guarantees that all our flight arrangements and flight-inclusive packages are 100% financially protected.

Why Should I book holidays with Fly&Joy?

Fly&Joy's portfolio of holiday packages offers something for almost every type of traveller. From multi-center holidays to meticulously planned family getaways, we provide a wide variety of holidays. In addition, Fly&Joy allows you to secure these grand experiences with flexible instalments. Contact our travel agency for more information today!

What holiday destinations do you offer?

No matter where you want to travel, we can take you there! Fly&Joy offers holiday packages and fares to a vast range of destinations. Our deals include both popular tourist hotspots and unique locations that are off the beaten path.

What are the payment methods accepted by Fly&Joy?
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Bank transfer
  • Cash payments in our office
  • Card payments can be done through the E-pay link by the customer.

Card payments can be done through the E-pay link by the customer. Additionally, your consultant may also advise you about the most appropriate payment method and the process involved.
